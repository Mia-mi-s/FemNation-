Because Silence Isn't Neutral

"Feminism is the radical notion that women are people." — Marie Shear

Welcome to FemNation, a space created to learn, question, and share ideas about feminism its history, its struggles, and its victories. Here, we explore what equality truly means and why the feminist movement is still a vital necessety today.

Click to croll →

Feminism is not just a movement it's a voice, a history, and a promise of equality.

It's about recognizing that every person, regardless of gender, deserves the same respect, rights, and opportunities.

This website explores:

  • What feminism really means.
  • How it has evolved through different waves.
  • The global voices that continue to fight for equality.
  • The myths and misinformation that still surround it.
Did You Know? Click to Learn
  • 650 million women alive today were married as children. Child marriage remains widespread in South Asia and Sub-Saharan Africa. It violates basic human rights and disrupts education and health.
  • Women earn about 77 cents for every dollar earned by men. The global gender pay gap is still 23%, and only 1% of companies worldwide have fully closed it.
  • 132 million girls are out of school. Barriers include poverty, conflict, and early marriage.
  • 1 in 3 women experience physical or sexual violence. Many face abuse from partners, and in conflict zones sexual violence is often used as a weapon.
  • Over 1.2 billion women and girls live where safe abortion access is restricted.
  • FemNation is a student-made project for the SIO 2025 class to raise awareness. It's not only about learning — it's about inspiring reflection.

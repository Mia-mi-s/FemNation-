Feminism Today

Equality, rights, and inclusion

Feminism today is more inclusive than ever. It focuses on:

  • Equal opportunities
  • Control over the body
  • Representation in media
  • Shared family roles
  • Ending gender-based violence

Social media helps spread awareness. Movements like #MeToo show how one voice can create global change.

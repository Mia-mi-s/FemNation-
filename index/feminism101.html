The feminist movement addresses various forms of inequality:

  • **Economic Equality:** Closing the **gender pay gap** and ensuring equal opportunities for leadership.
  • **Reproductive Rights:** Ensuring bodily autonomy and access to healthcare.

  • **Political Representation:** Achieving equal representation in government and decision-making roles.
  • **Challenging Norms:** Dismantling harmful **gender stereotypes** for all people, including men.
